Infrared Image Colorization & Enhancement

for Improved Object Interpretation

Transforming low-visibility infrared satellite imagery into high-fidelity RGB reconstructions using deep learning.

Core Objectives

Enhancement

Improve visibility of faint edges, textures and structures.

Super Resolution

Recover fine spatial details from low quality infrared imagery.

RGB Reconstruction

Generate realistic visible-spectrum imagery from infrared inputs.

Semantic Integrity

Preserve object structure and prevent false interpretations.
